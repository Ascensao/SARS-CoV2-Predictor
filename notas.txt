the app uses the percentage change in the number of infected cases over the past few days to make a prediction for the current and future days. The app calculates the prediction using a simple average of the percentage change over the past few days, with the option to adjust the rate of increase/decrease in the predicted value using an input from the user.

It seems like a straightforward approach, but keep in mind that this model may not be accurate for the long term, as there are many factors that could affect the spread of the virus. Also, the model does not take into account any external factors, such as the number of people vaccinated or any changes in government policy that may affect the rate of infection.
